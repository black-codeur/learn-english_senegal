Conditional Sentences Exercises

Exercise 1: First Conditional

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in parentheses:

  1. If it (rain), we will stay at home.
  2. If you (study), you will pass the exam.
  3. If she (invite) me, I will come to her party.
  4. If they (be) late, we will start without them.
  5. If you (ask) nicely, they will help you.
  6. If he (get) a good job, he will move to a bigger house.
  7. If we (not/leave) now, we will miss the bus.
  8. If I (win) the lottery, I will travel the world.
  9. If you (not/hurry), you will be late for the meeting.
  10. If they (not/call), we won't know what to do.

Exercise 2: Second Conditional

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in parentheses:

  1. If I (have) more money, I would buy a new car.
  2. If she (be) younger, she would travel more.
  3. If we (live) in a bigger house, we would invite more people.
  4. If you (study) harder, you would get better grades.
  5. If they (not/eat) so much, they would feel better.
  6. If he (play) more often, he would become a better player.
  7. If I (win) a million dollars, I would donate to charity.
  8. If she (not/lose) the keys, we would have entered the house.
  9. If you (be) taller, you would play basketball better.
  10. If they (move) to the city, they would find better jobs.

Exercise 3: Third Conditional

Complete the sentences with the correct form of the verb in parentheses:

  1. If I (know) about the party, I would have gone.
  2. If they (arrive) earlier, they would have met the famous actor.
  3. If you (study) more, you would have passed the exam.
  4. If she (not/miss) the bus, she would have been on time.
  5. If we (take) a taxi, we would have arrived earlier.
  6. If he (be) more careful, he wouldn't have broken the vase.
  7. If I (see) you, I would have said hello.
  8. If you (not/forget) the tickets, we would have gone to the concert.
  9. If they (not/get) lost, they would have arrived on time.
  10. If we (know) the answer, we would have told you.
